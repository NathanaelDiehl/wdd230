Full Time Missionaries

We offer extended stay rooms for our beloved Full time temple missionaries. Each room is equipped with a kitchen including: a sink, refrigerator, stove, dishwasher, and microwave. These rooms also include additional storage and space for gear and clothing.

Full time temple Missionaries are what help us continue the work of the lord, so we want to help in return by offering select suites at discounted prices for our missionaries. each of these suites have disability access as well as adjustments for ease of use.
