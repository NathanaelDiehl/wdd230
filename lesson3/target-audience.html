Target Audience:

Our target audience will include big and small business owners and their investors. We want to include entrepreneurs, agents, and private contractors to our listings to help build a up and down chain of contacts and connections. Our guidelines will also promote anyone who wants to build a friendly and supportive community that will uplift and promote businesses either starting our or in long standing within the community.

Personas:

Nathanael Diehl

Co-founder of Idaho Falls Chamber, Nathanael Diehl has been in the business of promoting healthy and vibrant community business growth. Over the last 10 years he has helped give over thirteen small companies the footing they needed to flourish in the Idaho Falls region. He has also provided networking opportunities to dozens of private contractors.

Miranda Sorensen

Member Miranda Sorensen has been a local investor for the past 20 years. Having gained valuable insight from several investing firms, she has guided several of our members into thriving investments that have helped both our local revenue grow and for helping new businesses open. With a network of trusted collaborators, Miranda has been a huge asset to both our chamber as well as our community.

Scenarios:

  • Scenario 1: What are the local businesses like?
  • Scenario 2: Where can I go to see what businesses in the area would be great to invest in?
  • Scenario 3: I am trying to find local private contractors for various activities. Where can I go to find them?
  • Scenario 4: How can IFC help me start my business in the Idaho Falls area?
  • Scenario 5: What can IFC help my business do? What type of networking do they offer?
