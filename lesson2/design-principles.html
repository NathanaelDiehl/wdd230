Hick's Law

Zomato

Zomato.com

Hick's law states that the more options are available to a person, the longer it will take for him or her to make a decision about which option is best. with Zomato it is designed to give the user simple and directive options so that the time to debate between choices is minimized.

Contrast

Onthegrid

onthegrid.city

Contrasting colors is very evident in OnTheGrid.City website. it uses opposite coloring for tabs and information. when you scroll over a section it reverses the colors creating a eye popping look.

Alignment

JT Garuke

jtgrauke.com

JT Grauke's website is a great example of left alignment. from the very beginning it shows an opening description that is simple and explanative while being clean and easy to read. the website aligns itself to have aligned paragraphs for easy reading.
